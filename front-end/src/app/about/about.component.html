Mountain | \ˈmount(ə)n\ | A region characterized by remoteness and inaccessibility

Pine | \ˈpīn\ | To yearn deeply for

My name is Josh and I'm the founder of MTN Pine. Mountains to me breathe a sense of beauty, danger, excitement and adventure into my soul. It's something I truly long for. They are where I'm reminded of purpose and where I take inspiration to create.

I’m also a sucker for the sentimental. A souvenir from a foreign travel, my grandfather’s old army chest, or a pair of shoes I held onto far too long; all items that hold a unique story. Other than the shoes, I want to pass those items and meaningful stories along.

This is why I create. When I craft a leather piece I do so hoping that it will be a means of inspiration for adventure and a vessel for a story; something meaningful that can be passed on.
